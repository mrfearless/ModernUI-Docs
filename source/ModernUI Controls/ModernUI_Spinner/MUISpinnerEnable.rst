========================
MUISpinnerEnable 
========================

MUISpinnerEnable, hWin::ref:`MUIWND<MUIWND>`

Enables the spinner animation and shows the ModernUI_Spinner control

**Parameters**

* [in] **hWin** - handle to the ModernUI_Spinner control

**Return**

None

**Example**

::

   Invoke MUISpinnerEnable, hSpinner
